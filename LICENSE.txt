The following libraries were incorporated into psi-toolkit:

* MPFD Parser by Grigory D Gorelov (GPL) - http://grigory.info/MPFDParser/
* UTF8-CPP (specific licence, see utf8/utf8.h) -
  http://utfcpp.sourceforge.net/
* A Maximum Entropy Modeling Toolkit for Python and C++ by Le Zhang -
  http://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/lzhang10/maxent.html
* sundown (for processing markdown) - https://github.com/tanoku/sundown

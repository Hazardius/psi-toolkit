        <h2>Project description</h2>

        <p>

            The aim of the project is to develop a tool chain (called <strong>psi-toolkit</strong>)
            for automatic processing of Polish (and - to lesser extent - other languages: English, German,
            French, Spanish and Russian) with the focus on machine translation. The tool chain will include:
        </p>
        <ul>
            <li>segmentation/tokenization/lemmatization,</li>
            <li>shallow parsing,</li>
            <li>deep parsing,</li>
            <li>rule-based machine translation,</li>

            <li>statistical machine translation,</li>
            <li>automatic generation of inflected forms from lemma sequences</li>
            <li>automatic postedition.</li>
        </ul>
